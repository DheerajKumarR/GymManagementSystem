Ronnie Coleman

Ronnie Coleman, the undisputed "King" of bodybuilding, born on May 13, 1964, in Monroe, Louisiana, left an indelible mark on the sport with a record-setting eight consecutive Mr. Olympia titles from 1998 to 2005. Known for his unmatched size, dedication, and iconic catchphrase "Yeah, buddy! Light weight, baby!", Coleman's colossal presence and relentless work ethic have become legendary in the iron game. Beyond the stage, his influence continues to inspire a generation of lifters, making him a true titan in the world of bodybuilding.
